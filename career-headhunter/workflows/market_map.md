# Workflow — Market Map

## Trigger

Use this workflow when the candidate is unsure which roles are worth targeting.

## Steps

1. Read Candidate Brief.
2. Identify 3-7 plausible role families.
3. For each role family, search sample postings or analyze representative job descriptions.
4. Compare candidate evidence against market requirements.
5. Classify each family as Primary, Adjacent, Stretch, or Excluded.
6. Update Candidate Brief and Search Strategy.
7. Recommend where to focus search effort.
