# 06 — Feedback Loop

## Feedback Questions

After each shortlist or evaluation, ask:

1. Which roles feel genuinely interesting?
2. Which roles would you reject immediately?
3. Are the scores too conservative, too generous, or accurate?
4. Should any job titles be added or excluded?
5. Should any sectors, geographies, or company types be added or excluded?
6. Should compensation, remote-work, relocation, or travel constraints change?
