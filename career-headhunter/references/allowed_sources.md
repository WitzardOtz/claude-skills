# Allowed and Preferred Sources

## Preferred Sources

- Company career pages.
- Reputable job boards.
- Permitted job-search APIs.
- Recruiter posts when publicly accessible.
- User-provided job links.
- User-provided emails, PDFs, and job descriptions.
- Professional networks when accessed manually or via permitted interfaces.

## Use with Caution

- Aggregators with stale listings.
- Reposted jobs without original company page.
- Anonymous recruiter ads.
- Jobs without compensation, location, or clear responsibilities.
- Jobs that appear copied across many websites.

## Avoid

- Prohibited scraping.
- Automated access that violates platform terms.
- Unverified compensation claims.
- Listings without source date or identifiable employer.

## Source Logging

For every externally sourced role, record:

- date accessed;
- source name;
- source URL;
- company;
- job title;
- notes on reliability or uncertainty.
