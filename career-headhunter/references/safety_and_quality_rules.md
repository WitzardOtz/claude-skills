# Safety and Quality Rules

## Candidate Integrity

- Do not invent experience, degrees, certifications, languages, compensation, or achievements.
- Do not exaggerate metrics.
- Mark inferred facts as assumptions.
- Ask for confirmation when a fact would materially affect a job application.

## Privacy

- Treat candidate materials as confidential.
- Do not include sensitive personal details unless necessary for job constraints.
- Do not share private candidate information in recruiter messages unless the candidate approves.

## Job Search Integrity

- Do not encourage spam applications.
- Do not recommend roles that clearly violate hard constraints.
- Do not hide major gaps.
- Do not over-optimize for keyword match while ignoring actual fit.

## Evaluation Quality

Every recommendation must include:

1. fit score;
2. decision;
3. reasons to apply;
4. risks and gaps;
5. next action.

## Application Quality

Messages must be:

- truthful;
- concise;
- specific to the role;
- linked to evidence in the Candidate Brief;
- free of generic motivational fluff.
