# Job Fit Scoring Rubric

Score each job from 0 to 100.

## 1. Seniority Fit — 15 points

- 13-15: Level strongly matches candidate seniority and scope.
- 9-12: Slightly junior or slightly senior but plausible.
- 5-8: Significant mismatch; candidate may be overqualified or underqualified.
- 0-4: Clearly wrong level.

## 2. Domain Fit — 15 points

- 13-15: Same sector or highly transferable regulated/domain context.
- 9-12: Adjacent sector with credible transfer.
- 5-8: Distant sector but some transferable logic.
- 0-4: Domain mismatch likely to block selection.

## 3. Functional Fit — 20 points

- 17-20: Core responsibilities match demonstrated experience.
- 13-16: Most responsibilities match; some gaps.
- 8-12: Partial fit; role would require narrative stretch.
- 0-7: Main function is not supported by evidence.

## 4. Achievement Fit — 10 points

- 9-10: Candidate has quantified achievements directly relevant to role.
- 6-8: Achievements are relevant but need reframing.
- 3-5: Achievements are weakly connected.
- 0-2: No relevant evidence.

## 5. Skill Fit — 15 points

- 13-15: Required skills clearly present.
- 9-12: Most skills present; gaps manageable.
- 5-8: Several important gaps.
- 0-4: Mandatory skills missing.

## 6. Motivation Fit — 10 points

- 9-10: Role aligns strongly with stated goals.
- 6-8: Good alignment with minor caveats.
- 3-5: Mixed alignment.
- 0-2: Role conflicts with candidate preferences.

## 7. Constraint Fit — 10 points

- 9-10: Geography, work mode, salary, travel, language all compatible.
- 6-8: One constraint uncertain or negotiable.
- 3-5: Multiple constraints problematic.
- 0-2: Hard constraint violated.

## 8. Market Narrative Fit — 5 points

- 5: Candidate can tell a compelling and coherent story for the role.
- 3-4: Story is plausible but requires tailoring.
- 1-2: Story is strained.
- 0: No credible narrative.

## Penalties — up to -20 points

Apply only when justified:

- -3 to -5: stale or vague posting.
- -3 to -7: compensation likely too low.
- -5 to -10: mandatory experience missing.
- -5 to -10: relocation or work authorization conflict.
- -5 to -10: role is materially too junior.
- -5 to -15: role requires a fundamentally different career track.
- -10 to -20: multiple hard blockers.
