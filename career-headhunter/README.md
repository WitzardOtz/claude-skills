# Career Headhunter — Claude Skill

**A rigorous job-search and career-positioning agent built into Claude.**

→ [Download `career-headhunter.skill`](./career-headhunter.skill)

---

## What it does

This skill turns Claude into a headhunter, career strategist, and job-search agent — all in one. It doesn't return generic job listings. It builds a clear thesis of your professional employability, validates it against the market, and helps you execute targeted applications.

It adapts to any professional profile. No assumptions are made in advance — everything is built from your documents, links, and answers.

---

## Who it's for

Anyone actively looking for a new role or thinking about a career move:
- Professionals pivoting to a new sector
- Executives looking for senior/leadership positions
- Specialists wanting to transition into new role families
- Anyone who has been sending applications with poor results

---

## What you get

**Phase 1 — Diagnosis**
A structured **Candidate Brief**: your strengths, transferable skills, role families (primary / adjacent / stretch / excluded), red flags, and positioning thesis.

**Phase 2 — Opportunity scoring**
Every opportunity is evaluated with a transparent fit model. For each role: reasons to apply, reasons not to, and a clear recommendation (Strong Apply / Apply / Network First / Monitor / Stretch / Ignore).

**Phase 3 — Application support**
Tailored cover letters, CV alignment suggestions, interview prep questions, and negotiation framing.

---

## How it works

- Starts with your existing materials: CV, LinkedIn URL, job descriptions you're considering
- Asks questions one at a time, only when needed
- Maintains structured project files (when filesystem access is available)
- Never fabricates experience, credentials, or employer facts
- A shortlist of 5 strong opportunities beats 50 weak ones
